🌦 Weather & Farming Guide

Smart farming depends on smart weather decisions

☀️

Sunny Weather

Good for harvesting crops and drying grains.

💡 Water crops early morning or evening.
🌧

Rainy Weather

Best for crop growth but avoid fertilizer use.

💡 Ensure proper drainage in fields.
☁️

Cloudy Weather

Moderate temperature helps sensitive crops grow.

💡 Ideal time for planting seeds.
❄️

Cold / Snowy Weather

Can damage crops if not protected.

💡 Use greenhouse or crop covers.
🌫

Hazy Weather

Low visibility and moisture in air.

💡 Avoid spraying pesticides.
